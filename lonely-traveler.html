Lonely Traveler

A mobile application designed to empower solo women travelers and connect like-minded adventurers.

Lonely Traveler is a multi-functional app designed to enhance the solo-female traveling experience. This is done by connecting female travelers with like-minded people and creating a resource for them to share their experiences with one another.

Problem

Traveling alone as a woman can be difficult and at times dangerous. In today’s day and age, it’s challenging for a woman to meet new people in new places and feel safe doing so. There are some resources out there for people to find information about the best spots to hit at any travel destination, or tour groups to pay for and join, but not many resources specifically for women travelers looking to travel alone and meet fellow female travelers along the way.

Solution

The Lonely Traveler mobile app streamlines the solo travel experience. It serves as a resource for any solo trips that users are embarking on, but it is also a way to connect with other women travelers. It is designed to give solo women travelers the safety net of a network of fellow adventurers.
